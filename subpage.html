About Us

A company that connects car collectors to the people,sites and organizations that sell or auction collector cars.Based in Nairobi we outsource most of the cars due to Kenya banning importation of old cars.

We attend auctions and bid on behalf of our customers and make sure they get a good deal and whatever car they get is in the condition that was stipulated in the advertisements. We deliver the car to the customer anywhere in the world and we handle all the paper work..

The company was started by 4 friends and lovers of classic cars who were tired of getting ripped off and getting cars that were basically un-restorable
